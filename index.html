How It Works

1. Choose one golfer from each of the five groups below.

2. Your golfers will earn points based on their performance:

  • 1st in group: 5 points
  • 2nd in group: 4 points
  • 3rd in group: 3 points
  • 4th in group: 2 points
  • 5th in group: 1 point
  • Missed cut: -1 point
  • Pick the tournament winner: +5 bonus points
  • 2 bonus points if your golfer is leading after any round

3. Ties will split points (e.g. 2 tied players for 1st = 2.5 pts each).

4. Entry is €10. Winner takes all — ties split the prize.
